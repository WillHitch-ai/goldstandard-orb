Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains how the Gold Standard ORB System collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit goldstandardorb.com or sign up to receive our free guide. We comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

The Gold Standard ORB System is operated as a sole trader business based in the United Kingdom. Contact: hello@goldstandardorb.com

What data we collect

We collect only what you voluntarily provide:

  • Email address — when you sign up for the free London Profiles guide or join the mailing list

We do not collect payment details (handled entirely by Gumroad) and do not use tracking cookies or advertising scripts.

How we use your data

  • Delivering the free guide you requested
  • Sending occasional updates — new content, product availability, system updates

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party for marketing purposes.

Legal basis for processing

We process your email on the basis of consent. You may withdraw consent at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Third-party services

  • Kit (ConvertKit) — email marketing. Your email is stored on Kit's servers per their privacy policy at kit.com/privacy.
  • Gumroad — product sales. Purchase data handled entirely by Gumroad. We do not receive or store payment details.
  • ImprovMX — domain email forwarding. No subscriber data passes through ImprovMX.

Data retention

Your email is retained while you remain subscribed. Unsubscribing removes it within 30 days. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us directly.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or erase your data; restrict or object to processing; withdraw consent; and lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk. Email hello@goldstandardorb.com to exercise any right — we respond within 30 days.

Cookies and tracking

This website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. Standard server logs may record IP addresses but are not used to identify individuals or shared with third parties.

Children

This site and its products are not directed at anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top will reflect any changes.

This policy covers goldstandardorb.com and our email list only. It does not apply to linked third-party sites including TradingView, FTMO, or Amazon.
