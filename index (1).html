The session
How a session works

Three phases.
Twenty minutes of active decisions.

Before 13:30 UTC
Pre-session preparation
Check the calendar. Classify the London profile. Identify daily levels. Confirm bias. The entire session context is established before the range begins.
13:30 – 13:45 UTC
Opening range forms
Observe only. The first 15 candles define the OR high and low. Read ATR. Run the filter stack. You have a tradeable session or you stand aside — the decision is mechanical.
13:45 – 14:45 UTC
Trade window
Wait for a qualifying breakout. Enter on retest and reclaim. Stop placed structurally. Move to breakeven at 1R. Hold to 2.5R or named liquidity level. Max two trades.
14:45 UTC
Session closes — hard stop
No new entries regardless of setup quality. Log the session. The discipline of the hard close is part of what makes the system work over time.
